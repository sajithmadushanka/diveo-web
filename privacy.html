Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Diveo (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you play the Diveo mobile game.

1. Information Collection

Diveo does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may collect limited non-personal information automatically to help improve gameplay and app stability. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Gameplay statistics (such as scores or session duration)
  • Crash and performance data

2. Use of Information

Non-personal information is used only to:

  • Improve game performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand general gameplay behavior
  • Display advertisements (if enabled)

Diveo does not sell or share personal information.

3. Advertising

Diveo may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect and use non-personal information (for example, device identifiers or approximate location) to provide and measure ads, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Diveo does not control how third-party advertisers collect or use this data.

4. Analytics and Game Services

Diveo may use third-party analytics tools to better understand how players interact with the game. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous and is used only to improve the game experience.

Diveo may also use platform services such as Apple Game Center or Google Play Games Services for leaderboards. These services are operated by their respective platform providers and are subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device, such as:

  • High scores
  • Game preferences (for example, sound settings)

This data is not transmitted to our servers and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

Diveo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information unintentionally, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

7. Third-Party Services

Diveo may use third-party services for advertising, analytics, or platform features. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
